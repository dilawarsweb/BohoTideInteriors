Our Story

Bringing Boho-Chic to American Homes

Founded in 2015, Boho Tide Interiors was born from a passion for free-spirited design and a desire to help homeowners create spaces that reflect their unique personalities. Our founder, Maya Rivers, spent years traveling the world, collecting textiles, artifacts, and inspiration from cultures across the globe.

We believe your home should tell your story. Our team of experienced designers specializes in blending vibrant patterns, natural materials, and eclectic treasures to create spaces that feel both curated and comfortable. From cozy apartments to sprawling suburban homes, we bring the boho-chic aesthetic to life across the United States.

At Boho Tide, we prioritize sustainability, sourcing ethically-made pieces and incorporating vintage finds whenever possible. Our design philosophy embraces imperfection, celebrates individuality, and creates environments that nourish the soul.

Cookie Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

This Cookie Policy explains how Boho Tide Interiors uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas.

Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. This information is aggregated and anonymous.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced, more personal features.

Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement.

3. Specific Cookies We Use

Below is a list of cookies we use and their purposes:

  • session_id: Essential cookie for user session management
  • _ga: Google Analytics cookie for distinguishing users
  • _gid: Google Analytics cookie for distinguishing users
  • _gat: Google Analytics cookie for throttling request rate
  • cookie_consent: Remembers your cookie preferences
  • preferences: Stores your website preferences

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for sharing content
  • Advertising networks for targeted advertising
  • Payment processors for transaction processing

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

5. Cookie Duration

Cookies can be either session cookies or persistent cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until deleted

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

Browser Controls

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually find these settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain types of cookies, particularly advertising cookies, through industry opt-out programs:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): optout.aboutads.info
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): optout.networkadvertising.org

Google Analytics Opt-Out

To opt out of Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

7. Consequences of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for basic website functionality.
